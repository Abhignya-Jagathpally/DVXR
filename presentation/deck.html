The framework

One system, many signals

  • EEG + PHR + PPG flow into a single availability-aware model.
  • Two clinical targets from shared representation: glucose and mind-state.
  • Modalities are complementary, not siloed — one pipeline, many devices.
Goal 1 · Ingestion

The pipeline ingests all five modalities

  • Five modalities land in one canonical schema — EEG, PPG/wearable, EHR, CGM, behavioral.
  • Plug-and-play encoders per device; missing streams handled by design.

Behavioral metrics are wired into the pipeline, not yet run end-to-end.

Model in detail

Availability-aware mixture-of-experts

  • Each modality has a dedicated expert encoder.
  • A gate weighs only the experts whose signals are present for a subject.
  • Graceful degradation: partial device sets still yield a prediction.
Goal 2 · Result — glucose

Beats persistence at every horizon

  • RMSE 12.8 / 21.9 / 26.6 / 29.1 mg/dL @ 30 / 60 / 90 / 120 min.
  • Superiority gate over persistence passed at all four horizons.
  • Evaluated patient-held-out — no leakage across subjects.

Goal 3 · Why this model?

The causal representation is the win — not depth

  • Climbing the model ladder, gains come from the causal representation, not deeper nets.
  • Bigger/deeper baselines do not close the gap.
  • Interpretable by construction — the mechanism, not the parameter count, carries performance.
Which device matters

Per-device leave-one-out

  • Each device dropped in turn to measure its marginal contribution.
  • Quantifies which streams are load-bearing vs. redundant.
  • Guides a minimal, deployable sensor set.

Trust

Causal · calibrated · abstains · guarded · never prescribes

Causal
Predictions rest on a causal representation, not spurious correlation.
Calibrated
Confidence is calibrated so probabilities mean what they say.
Abstains
Declines to answer when signals are insufficient rather than guessing.
LLM explains (guarded)
Language explanations are guarded — grounded in model outputs, not free-form claims.
Never prescribes
Outputs risk, never a diagnosis or a treatment decision.
